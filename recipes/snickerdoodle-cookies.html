Snickerdoodle Cookies

Description:

Snickerdoodles are a cookie unlike any other; visually appealing, delicious, and simple to make. This recipe will produce cookies that just melt in your mouth, leaving the intoxicating taste of buttery, cinnoman-sugar gooodness in its wake.

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 stick unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla

    • Cinnamon-Sugar Mixture

  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
